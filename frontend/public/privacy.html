Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-14 · Version 1.0

1. Who we are

uSTAT is a free, browser-based statistical analysis platform created and operated by Dr. Yusuf Hoşoğlu. Contact: adycovs@gmail.com.

2. What data we receive

  • The file you choose to upload (CSV, Excel, SPSS, SAS, Stata, or JSON session).
  • The HTTP request metadata your browser sends: IP address, user agent, referrer, timestamp, requested URL.
  • The analysis parameters you submit through the UI (e.g. selected variables, knot counts, group columns).

3. How your data is processed

All statistical computation happens on the uSTAT server, not in your browser. When you upload a file:

  • It is parsed in RAM by the backend and bound to a session identifier (session_id).
  • It is never written to disk. The dataframe lives only in process memory.
  • It is automatically cleared from memory 30 minutes after your last activity (configurable: SESSION_TTL_SECONDS = 1800 in backend/services/store.py).
  • If the server is restarted, all in-memory data is lost — there is no persistent storage.

The browser-side React app uses zustand for local UI state. Closing the tab clears that state. Nothing is saved to localStorage or cookies for your data.

4. What we log

  • Web server access log: standard HTTP metadata (IP, path, status, timestamp). Retained by Render for up to 30 days.
  • Code runner audit log (only when ENABLE_CODE_RUNNER=1): timestamp, IP, session_id, SHA-256 prefix of the submitted code, first 200 characters, duration, exit code. Your dataset is never logged.
  • MapMyVisitors widget: aggregated visitor map shown on the splash screen. See their privacy notice at mapmyvisitors.com. Disable by blocking third-party scripts.

5. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or share your data.
  • We do not have user accounts, profiles, or persistent identifiers.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics beyond the visitor map.
  • We do not run automated decision-making or profiling.

6. Data subject to regulation

uSTAT is not HIPAA, GDPR, or ISO 27001 certified. Do not upload Protected Health Information (PHI), personally identifiable information (PII), or any data whose handling is restricted by law or by your institution. Anonymise datasets before upload: strip names, MRNs, dates of birth, and free-text identifiers. For regulated workflows, request a self-hosted or local-only build via email.

7. Your rights

Because we hold no persistent user data, the standard GDPR access / portability / erasure rights have nothing to act on once your session expires. If you wish to delete a session immediately, close the tab — the backend will discard it within 30 minutes of inactivity.

8. Security

See the Security page and the public SECURITY.md in the source repository for the threat model, sandbox hardening, and the responsible-disclosure contact.

9. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be announced in the in-app Changelog (About → Changelog) and through the Last updated date above.
